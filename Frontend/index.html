How It Works

Simple four-step process to report and resolve campus issues

📸
1
Spot the Issue
Notice a broken fan, window, projector or any campus problem? Click a photo on the spot.
📝
2
File a Complaint
Login with your enrollment ID, fill the form with department, block, room number and upload the photo.
📨
3
HOD Notified
Your complaint instantly reaches the respective department head for review and action.
✅
4
Track Resolution
Monitor your complaint status — Pending, In Progress, or Resolved — right from your dashboard.
Note: This portal is exclusively for registered students and faculty of J.C. Bose University, YMCA, Faridabad.  |  For urgent issues contact your department office directly.
