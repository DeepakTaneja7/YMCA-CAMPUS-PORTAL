How to File a Complaint

Follow these steps for a quick resolution

  1. Click on "File Complaint" in the sidebar.
  2. Enter a clear title describing the problem.
  3. Select the Department responsible for the area.
  4. Choose the issue category (e.g., Electrical, Plumbing).
  5. Set the priority — High for urgent issues.
  6. Enter the Block and Room number precisely.
  7. Write a clear description of the problem.
  8. Upload photos of the issue (upto 5 images).
  9. Click "Submit Complaint" — done! 🎉
📌 Your complaint will be visible to the HOD of the selected department. You can track the status under "My Complaints".
