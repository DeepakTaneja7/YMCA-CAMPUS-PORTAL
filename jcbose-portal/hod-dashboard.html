HOD Guide

How to manage complaints on this portal

Managing Complaints

  1. You only see complaints filed for your department.
  2. Review each complaint — read description and check photos.
  3. Use the "Update Status" dropdown to change status:
    • Pending – Not yet acted upon
    • In Progress – Work has been initiated
    • Resolved – Issue has been fixed
    • Rejected – Complaint is invalid
  4. Click "Add Remark" to provide a response or resolution note to the student.
  5. Use filters to quickly find complaints by status or priority.
ℹ️ Students can see the status and your remarks in real time from their dashboard.

✏️ Add / Update Remark

Add a remark explaining the action taken, resolution, or reason for rejection. This will be visible to the student.
